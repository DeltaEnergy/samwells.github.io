RPD 6280

Oxford County Agricultural Systems Approach

This project examines agricultural systems planning in Oxford County, including agricultural land preservation, rural development pressures, and implementation of Ontario's Provincial Planning Statement (2024).

Project Overview

This report evaluates how Oxford County can strengthen its Agricultural Systems Approach while protecting prime farmland, supporting agricultural viability, and managing future growth.

Key themes include agricultural preservation, land-use planning, settlement boundary pressures, rural economic development, and long-term food system resilience.
